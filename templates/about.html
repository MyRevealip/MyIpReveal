Our Story

MyIPReveal.com was founded in 2023 by a team of network professionals and privacy advocates who recognized the need for a reliable, transparent, and privacy-focused IP address information service.

After experiencing frustration with existing IP lookup tools that were cluttered with excessive advertising, collected user data, or provided inaccurate information, we decided to create a solution that would offer users a clean, straightforward way to access accurate IP information while respecting their privacy.

Today, MyIPReveal.com serves thousands of users daily, providing essential network information tools without the intrusive practices common on many similar services.

Our Mission

At MyIPReveal.com, our mission is to:

Provide Accurate Information

Deliver precise and reliable IP address information using multiple verified data sources to ensure accuracy even during service outages.

Respect User Privacy

Maintain a strict no-logs policy for IP lookups and minimize data collection to only what's necessary for service operation.

Educate Users

Create accessible, informative content that helps users understand IP addresses, network concepts, and online privacy issues.

Innovate Continuously

Constantly improve our tools and develop new features to meet the evolving needs of users in an ever-changing internet landscape.

Our Technology

MyIPReveal.com employs a range of technologies to provide reliable and accurate IP information:

  • Multiple API Providers: We use several IP geolocation data sources with automatic failover to ensure consistent service availability.
  • Advanced Caching System: Our optimized caching reduces API calls and delivers faster results while reducing dependency on external services.
  • Responsive Design: The site is built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.
  • Performance Optimization: We employ modern web performance techniques including code minification, lazy loading, and browser caching to deliver a fast, efficient experience.

Our Privacy Commitment

At MyIPReveal.com, we believe that tools providing IP information should themselves respect user privacy. Our commitment includes:

Our Privacy Principles

  • No Logging of IP Lookups: We don't store records of the IP addresses you look up.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect the information necessary to provide our service.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We limit third-party scripts that could potentially track users across the web.
  • Transparency: Our privacy policy clearly explains what limited data we do collect and how it's used.
  • Educational Resources: We provide resources to help users protect their own privacy online.

For complete details about how we handle your information, please see our Privacy Policy.
